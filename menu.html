MENU


BK Restaurants is recognized for its impeccable quality, attention to detail and its vibrant atmosphere. Led by award-winning chefs, BK Restaurants brings together exquisite design, perfectly-prepared food and an exciting beverage program to create a contemporary establishment in the heart of the city. BK Restaurants has been designed with spacious seating and a beautiful veranda. It is known as one of the best restaurants in Los Angeles.

If you are looking for a memorable dining experience, then you should give Craft a visit. The secret to the success of BK Restaurants is our dynamic and seasonal menus. All foods are scratch-made and perfectly prepared. Our menus featuring everything from seasonal vegetables to freshly caught seafood, perfectly roast chicken and house made pastas. Every dish prepared to perfection combining new and traditional flavors for our guests. Residents and tourists alike, flock to Craft to indulge in our dynamic farm to table dining experience in Main Dining Room and for our vibrant happy hours! Our restaurant’s most popular feature is our patio, distinguishing us as one of the best outdoor restaurants in Los Angeles.

You can plan an entire business meeting or invite friends and family for an authentically local food experience. As we deliver on our promises of the best tasting foods and incredible freshness, our customers continue to show their support for one of the leading restaurants in the farm to table and sustainability movements.

We continue to set the standard and are recognized as the best restaurant in Los Angeles. Come enjoy one of the best lunches in Century City, our vibrant happy hour or a dynamic seasonal dinner at BK Restaurant today!
